Pressure and Salt Tracker

Privacy Policy

This website is a personal health tracking tool for logging blood pressure, sodium intake, food scans, and optional wellness data connections.

Information collected

  • Blood pressure readings, pulse, notes, and timestamps that you enter or import.
  • Food, barcode, sodium, and daily tracking data that you save.
  • Optional Fitbit data if you choose to connect your Fitbit account.

How the information is used

The information is used only to provide tracking, summaries, charts, and personal recordkeeping inside this application.

Sharing

Your information is not sold. Data may be stored with hosting and database providers used to run the application.

Fitbit connection

If you connect Fitbit, the app may access approved Fitbit account data such as steps, heart rate, sleep, and weight to display that information back to you in your dashboard.

Medical notice

This website is for personal tracking and organization only. It is not medical advice and should not replace professional care.

Contact
